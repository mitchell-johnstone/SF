# Song (working title)

I can't use the words about time. It shouldn't exist for them yet. So they can't "wait". One moment simply is all.

Nwo snapped the branch across his thigh, a reality splintering. Nwo threw the pieces onto the bonfire that was death, reality consuming. Nwo looked into the sky and saw hope racing across the sky, silhouetted by the tree that was time.
